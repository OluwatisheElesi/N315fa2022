Design

No matter what you do as an artist, everything comes down to design. If your art isn’t “hitting” the way you want it to, chances are you ignored one of the key Design Principles. Once you learn how to turn your artistic eye toward these elements, you’ll never look at any art the same way again.

2D Animation

A picture is worth a thousand words, so how many more paragraphs can you get with 24 pictures per second? Animation isn’t just about bringing art to life; it’s about emotion and storytelling as well. This isn’t just crafting cartoon characters and manipulating them in a virtual set. Everything from title sequences to iPhone apps can benefit from a firm understanding of the Principles of Animation.

3D Animation

If you’re a 2D artist looking to step up into a new level, add 3D to your resume. While many of the skills you already possess can transfer over, you’ll have to develop some new muscles to really be able to flex in this world. Fortunately, we’ve got a curriculum designed to get you from beginner to master.
